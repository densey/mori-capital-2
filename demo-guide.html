05 · Compliance & trust

Built for an EU-regulated asset manager

Industry-standard regulatory and consent UX expected by institutional and retail investors visiting EU fund websites — covered out of the box.

Investor type gate

First-visit modal. Confirms the visitor is not a U.S. person before fund information is shown. Standard for EU AIFMD/MiFID-distributed funds.

  • Confirmation checkbox — "I am not a U.S. person"
  • Two role paths: Retail Investor / Professional / Institutional
  • Decline path with friendly fallback message
  • One-shot — remembered in localStorage
  • Backdrop blur, scroll lock, keyboard accessible

Cookie consent (GDPR)

Bottom slide-in banner shown after the gate is acknowledged. Two clear choices — no dark patterns.

  • "Accept all" · "Necessary only" buttons
  • Cookie Policy link inline
  • Decision saved in localStorage with timestamp
  • Mobile responsive — stacks to a single column
  • Versioned key (v1) for future re-consent

MFSA regulator badge

Permanently visible in the footer CTA strip — "Regulated by MFSA · Firm Reference C66999". Clicking opens the legal disclaimer page.

Regulated by MFSA · Firm Reference C66999

Footer regulatory disclaimer

Permanent block above copyright — covers MiFID authorisation, non-U.S. persons restriction, 1940 Act note, past-performance warning and prospectus reference.

  • MFSA C66999 + MiFID directive reference
  • Not registered under U.S. Investment Advisers Act of 1940
  • "Past performance is not a reliable indicator…" line

06 · Motion & interaction

Subtle motion, premium feel

Movement is used sparingly — reinforcing hierarchy and brand confidence rather than calling attention to itself. Every animation respects the user's prefers-reduced-motion setting.

Hero video loop

Self-hosted Mori cinematic clip (hero-final.mp4) plays muted on autoplay, loops seamlessly. Poster image fallback (hero-istanbul.jpg) displays during load. Easy to swap to a JS video gallery (already coded) if multiple clips return.

3D mouse-tilt card

Cinematic showcase. Card tilts up to ±9° following the cursor (lerp-smoothed via requestAnimationFrame). Three floating chips (multi-currency, 10Y annualised, MFSA UCITS) sit on different Z-depths for true parallax. Soft sin/cos idle drift when the cursor is away.

Sticky header transition

Header sits transparent over the hero video. Once the user scrolls past 600px, it locks to the top with a navy fill, the logo eases from 80→64px, and padding compresses — nothing jolts.

Reveal-on-scroll

WOW.js fades each section block in with staggered delays (0.1s · 0.2s · 0.4s). Headlines split into characters via SplitText for a controlled reveal. Counters animate from 0 to target on scroll into view.

Card hover

Insight cards lift 4px and adopt a teal border on hover. Fund cards animate the inner image with a 1.04 scale. Award champion card has a subtle inner glow on hover.

Demo-mode toast

Click intercept on sub-page links shows a small navy pill toast at the top: "Demo — sub-pages coming soon", auto-dismisses after 1.8s. Easily removed when full site is live.

08 · Demo notes

How to walk through the demo

A few things to know before opening index.html — especially that this is the homepage only, with sub-page navigation intentionally muted.

Suggested walkthrough

  1. Open in incognito — first-visit experience triggers the investor gate.
  2. Confirm "I am not a U.S. person" — both buttons unlock; pick Retail or Professional.
  3. Cookie banner appears after the gate dismisses — Accept all or Necessary only.
  4. Hero video plays muted, looped. Sticky header is transparent over it.
  5. Scroll past the hero — header locks to navy with a smooth logo shrink.
  6. Funds & Mori Style — theme animations reveal each block as it enters view.
  7. Cinematic showcase — hover the dashboard card to feel the 3D tilt; chips drift on different depths.
  8. Awards & Insights — cards lift on hover with a teal accent border.
  9. Team grid — six profiles, real photos pulled from the existing Mori site.
  10. Footer — full disclaimer + MFSA badge + Stay Informed newsletter.
  11. Resize the window down to a phone width — the header turns Mori-teal and the menu collapses to a drawer.

Demo limitations

  • Single page only — index.html
  • Sub-page links show a "coming soon" toast (intercepted in JS)
  • EN/DE switcher: only EN active for now
  • Live mailto / tel still work normally
  • Forms are decorative (no backend wiring yet)

Re-trigger the gate & cookie banner

Open DevTools console and run:

localStorage.clear();
location.reload();
